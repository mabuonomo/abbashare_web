#abbashare
simplifies the installation process of your ios apps (.ipa files), with adhoc or inhouse profiles, via browser,
without using TestFlight

With an AdHoc profile, you can install your test app only on devices that have the UUIDs you saved.
With an InHouse profile, you can install your test app on a lot more devices without having to worry about collecting UUIDs.
#abbashare works only with Safari on iOS.

#abbashare in action

Simply to start
Log with you dropbox account.
To start, simply drag&drop your ipas into the relative field
Open this link on a Safari on iOS (iPhone or iPad).
Do you have any doubts? See the tutorial video on youtube at the top of this page.

Example of administrator page

Storage, speed and more
#abbashare save your ipa files on your Drobox! No files are saved locally on server.
You can delete anytime this files.

Thanks to Dropbox you will can install your app in a few seconds.
